About Us

Welcome to Codojedzenia, a bakery haven situated in the vibrant city of Gothenburg, Sweden. For more than five years, we have been perfecting Polish pastry with a touch of Swedish perfection.

At Codojedzenia, we're not just a bakery; we're a celebration of culinary fusion and heartfelt dedication.

Our journey began with a profound love for crafting delectable cakes and pastries. With an unwavering passion for baking, we've mastered the art of creating indulgent treats that delight the senses and warm the soul. Each cake, every pastry, is an embodiment of our commitment to excellence and flavor innovation.

Nestled in the beating heart of Gothenburg, our restaurant embodies a warm, inviting atmosphere where every visitor is welcomed like family. Our dedicated team comprises culinary enthusiasts driven by a shared goal: to present you with an unforgettable dining experience. From our chefs meticulously infusing traditional Polish recipes with a touch of Swedish influence to our attentive staff providing exceptional service, we strive for culinary excellence in every dish we serve.

We take pride in sourcing high-quality ingredients, ensuring that every bite is a harmonious symphony of taste and freshness. Whether you're savoring our signature pastries or indulging in our diverse menu showcasing the best of both Polish and Swedish cuisines, expect nothing short of culinary brilliance.

At Codojedzenia, we believe in more than just serving food; we're passionate storytellers, weaving narratives through our dishes that reflect our love for culinary artistry. Join us on a gastronomic adventure that transcends borders and traditions, where every plate tells a tale of flavors, cultures, and heartfelt dedication.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. We invite you to immerse yourself in the flavors, the warmth, and the hospitality that define Codojedzenia.
